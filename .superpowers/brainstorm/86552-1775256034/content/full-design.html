Design Details

  • Top bar: Stats on left, category dropdown on right — single row, ~40px total
  • Progress bar: 2px thin line, barely visible but informative
  • Card: Fills all remaining vertical space (no fixed height). Grows/shrinks with screen size
  • Bottom sheet: iOS-style with drag handle, grouped categories, controls at bottom
  • Dropdown label: Shows currently selected category name(s), or "All Categories" when everything selected
  • Swipe gestures: Swipe left = Again, swipe right = Got it (on the card)
  • Tap outside sheet: Dismisses it (standard iOS pattern)
  • No title header on mobile: Wastes space — the app name isn't needed once you're in it
